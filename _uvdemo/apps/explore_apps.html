UVdesk - How to add an Apps?

What are Explore Apps?

Integrate Apps as per as your needs to get things done faster than ever.

Steps for adding apps-

  1. Login to the UVdesk Support Portal.
  2. On the Dashboard, you will see "APPS" menu and under it, you will find "Explore apps".


  3. Click on the "Explore apps" Icon and then you will be redirected to a new page.
  4. In the new page, you will see a search bar and along with the pre-installed apps (if any).
  5. Here you can add apps and save changes.

You can purchase the apps for your helpdesk from here.
